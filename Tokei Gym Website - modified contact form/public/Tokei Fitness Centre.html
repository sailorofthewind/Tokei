GYM

Welcome to Londons's best kept fitness secret, The Hidden Gym, where you can choose from a full range of cardiovascular equipment, resistance equipment, free weights and other pieces of equipment for variable exercises. In addition to free space for mat or body exercises. You can come and workout at the Hidden Gym every day where there is always a friendly atmosphere. We welcome members of all abilities, from the beginner to the more experienced. If you need a gym induction, just ask at reception.

FITNESS

Body Conditioning, Circuit Training, Zumba, Pilates and Yoga are amongst the list of timetabled Fitness classes. Many of these classes take place at lunch time so why not join us then.

MARTIAL ARTS

Aiki Ju Jitsu, San Shou Kuan, Judo, Karate, Olympic Freestyle Wrestling and Thai Boxing are amongst the list of timetabled Martial Arts classes. Some of these classes take place at lunch time so why not join us then.
